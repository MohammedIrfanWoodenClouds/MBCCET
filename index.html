About Us

Mar Baselios Christian College of Engineering & Technology, Kuttikkanam, Peermade, is a self-financing institution affiliated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by AICTE.

Since its inception in 2001, we have been on a steady path of growth. We emphasize teaching excellence and a wholesome learning experience, providing our students with the best facilities to make MBC a premier educational institution.

Vibrant Campus Life

Swastika & Thandava

Swastika is our flagship intercollegiate technical and cultural fest. Celebrated annually over two exciting days, it serves as a vibrant platform for innovation, creativity, talent, and collaboration, featuring technical competitions and interactive sessions.

Thandava is our celebrated Cultural College Fest. Get ready for a day packed with power performances, electrifying vibes, and main-character moments. This isn't just a fest — it's a full-blown experience.
